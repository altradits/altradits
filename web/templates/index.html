// The Socratic Charter

Three laws.
No exceptions.

Our AI Mentor doesn't tutor. It interrogates. Every panic, every segfault — treated as a landmark, not a failure.

01
Never give code.
The AI identifies the concept causing an error — never the fix. You write every character of production Go yourself.
02
Validate via sandbox.
Feedback is grounded in real-time E2B terminal output — stdout, stderr, exit codes. Not guesses about intent.
03
Encourage failure.
A nil pointer panic is a milestone. A segfault is progress. Fear of breaking things is the real enemy.
